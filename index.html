Stress Management by Aldo Gonzalez

Purpose

For this video, I interview some instructors at Full Sail University with the intent of learning what these developers have learned throughout their professional careers to help manage the stress that may arise in the workplace.

Conclusion

I concluded that the most important thing when managing stress is a good work life balance. It is vital because you need to be able to say enough is enough for the day and live your life outside of work. Some calming music or music you know through and through is something which helps with stress because it puts you in the flow of building.
